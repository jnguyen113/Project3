Conways Game of Life

Rules

  • If a cell is alive, and 2 or 3 of it's neighbors are also alive, the cell remains alive.
  • If a cell is alive and it has more than 3 alive neighbors, it dies of overcrowding.
  • If a cell is alive and it has fewer than 2 alive neighbors, it dies of loneliness.
  • If a cell is dead and it has exactly 3 neighbors alive it becomes alive again.

Controls

  • Left Mouse click on the grid turns cells alive
  • Right Mouse click on the grid turns cells dead
  • Middle Mouse click on the grid toggles cell state
